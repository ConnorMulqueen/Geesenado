CE442 Project
GEESENADO
Connor Mulqueen, Jared Gavin, Kevin Wu, James Hsu
ABOUT

GEESENADO is a top-down 2D game that takes place on the University at Buffalo campus where you play as a computer science professor with the objective of failing all students! Upon starting the game, you can choose from a selection of playable computer science professors. In game, you 'flunk' students by using non-violent tools such as rulers and paper against the students in somewhat violent ways!

This project is powered by Unity Engine and built for CSE442.

LORE

A long time ago in city very, very close...the first goose was born. This goose had the ability to summon more geese to fly along alongside itself. It soon learned of its ability as a student of the University at Buffalo attempt to attack it and spawned it first geese-sidekick. The swarm then grew as the goose was overwhelmed with its power and the GEESENADO was born! Hearing of this incoming Geesenado the faculty members at the University at Buffalo decided all the students need to be evacuated, but they did not want to alarm the students. They decided to flunk all the students so they would have to change schools to avoid the incoming disaster!
